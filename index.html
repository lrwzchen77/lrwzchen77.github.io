About

I am an undergraduate student (Class of 2027) at the School of Software Engineering, Xinjiang University, with a GPA of 3.65/5.0 (top 4% in my major).

During the winter of 2025-2026, I participated in the Tsinghua University AIR Winter Camp, where I worked on Embodied AI and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, focusing on long-term memory mechanisms inspired by the human brain hippocampus-cortex architecture.

My research interests span three main directions:

  1. Embodied AI & VLA Models: Developing brain-inspired dual-layer memory architectures for long-horizon modeling, integrating motion vectors with temporal contrastive learning for self-supervised consolidation.

  2. Statistical Learning & Decision Frameworks: Building hierarchical decision frameworks combining linear mixed-effects models, survival analysis, and entropy-weighted TOPSIS for multi-criteria optimization.

  3. High-dimensional Data Analysis: Designing multi-stage intelligent analysis pipelines for compositional data, including CLR transformation, PCA dimensionality reduction, and enhanced K-Means clustering.

I have published three first-author papers at IEEE/CCF conferences (ABCM 2026, ICIC 2026, CITSC 2026). I am proficient in C, Python, PyTorch, LaTeX, and Cursor.
